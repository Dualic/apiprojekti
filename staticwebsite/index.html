Welcome to our website!

There are several paths that have different functions. Here is a quick guide how to use this API.

/pub/name_of_the_pub

Gives a GET-response. Insert the name of the pub to view the next time when a ICEYE satellite passes over that pub.

Example use:

/pub/GravelBottomCraftBrewery

/pubday/name_of_the_pub

Gives GET-response. Insert the name of the pub to view how many ICEYE satellites will pass over that pub over the next day.

Example use:

/pubday/TorrentBrewingCo

/find

Needs a POST. Give it the approximate longitude and latitude coordinates. Returns the name of a close by pub or says there are no close by pubs. Check slightly over 300 km radius (3.2 degrees in both coordinates).

Example POST JSON-payload:

{"longitude":-69.8, "latitude": 43.9}

Example linux CURL:

curl -X POST https://pub-api-gateway-8yny0kuq.ew.gateway.dev/find -H 'Content-Type: application/json' -d '{"longitude":-69.8, "latitude": 43.9}'
